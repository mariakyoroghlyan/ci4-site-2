About creation of Studio Shoo

STUDIO SHOO was founded by architect Shushana Khachatrian in 2017. The work we do accurately
represents an original way of thinking, approaching, and creating projects. The most meticulous attention is
paid to developing unique hotels, restaurants, and public interiors all over the world.

Our team
